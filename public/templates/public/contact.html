SUBWAY is considered the “healthy alternative” to the quick service offerings available today. The first SUBWAY sandwich shop was opened in Bridgeport, Connecticut in 1965 by company co-founder Fred DeLuca. In 1974, Fred and his business partner Dr. Peter Buck decided to begin franchising their restaurant, launching SUBWAY into tremendous growth, and providing almost unlimited opportunity for others to share in their prosperity!
